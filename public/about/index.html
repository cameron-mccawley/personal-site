About

Cameron McCawley is a senior studying computer science at Oregon State University, focusing on security.

Currently, he works as a security analyst for OSU, where he helps monitor and respond to a wide range of security incidents and threats.

He is also the president of the OSU Security Club, competing in many CTFs and CDC events under the team OSUSEC while also helping mangage and organize the weekly club meetings.
